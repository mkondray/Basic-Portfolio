About Me

I am a ski bum. I love to shred pow all day on the mountain and drink hot toddies on the chair lift. Super heavy powder days are my favorite because I can surf through the snow like it's a mondo wave. My favoite is skiing between the trees because its got the best powder and is very peaceful.

I teach kids how to tear up the trick park. Massive air is my favorite, but I do like to hit a rail here and there. I get all my gear for free because I am sponsored by some rad guys that dig my style. I like to eat cheeseburgers on the slopes as well as fruit roll ups. I carry a backpack full of extra goggles and replacement lenses as well as tons of other snacks and stuff.
